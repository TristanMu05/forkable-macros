Apex Eats — Tier Lists

Every restaurant and dish the team has ordered through Forkable. Click anything to vote.
How ranking works: everything starts in C tier. An entry's placement is the average of all its votes (💩 = 0 up to Willis ✨ = 7), rounded to the nearest tier — so one S vote and one 💩 vote average out to C, but two S votes against one 💩 pull it up to B. Votes aren't permanent: change yours anytime, or remove it by tapping your current pick again. Fresh votes can take a minute or two to show up for everyone else.

Day filter: which restaurants are orderable on which day is crowdsourced from the team browsing Forkable menus (via the macros extension) and resets every Saturday — days appear up top as soon as someone has browsed that day's menu.
